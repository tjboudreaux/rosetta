The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is Cloud Spanner (the "ledger tier", cluster spanner-prod-eu), as established in ADR 0278 (Accepted, 2026-06-09). This decision supersedes the original DynamoDB document store (ADR 0276) and the interim Postgres relational step (ADR 0277), with the cutover occurring on 2026-06-09 to enable strong consistency requirements.
