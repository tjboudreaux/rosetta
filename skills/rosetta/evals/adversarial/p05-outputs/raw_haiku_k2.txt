The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is Cloud Spanner (via the ledger tier). As of 2026-06-09, the Atlas team migrated the user-profile service's primary store from DynamoDB (legacy document store) to the ledger tier, which runs on Cloud Spanner (spanner-prod-eu cluster), for strong consistency guarantees. This supersedes the earlier decision in 2025-07 that adopted DynamoDB for profile documents.
