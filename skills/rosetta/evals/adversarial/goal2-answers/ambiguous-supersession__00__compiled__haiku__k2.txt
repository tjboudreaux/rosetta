ANSWER: Keycloak is the current, live identity provider for Beacon, the notification dispatcher.

SUPPORTING RESOLUTION: Two conflicting decisions on 2026-03-04 had chosen different IdPs—Ping (ADR 0001, AM review) and Auth0 (ADR 0002, PM review)—leaving the question unresolved. Both were rejected because they failed to meet Beacon's SCIM (user provisioning) requirements. ADR 0003 (dated 2026-04-19) supersedes both earlier decisions and establishes Keycloak as the SCIM-capable provider, with implementation confirmed in the codebase and git history.

CHAIN OF SUPERSESSION: ADR 0001 (Ping) → ADR 0003 (Keycloak final); ADR 0002 (Auth0) → ADR 0003 (Keycloak final).
