Raw, personally-identifiable EU payment logs are retained for 30 days post-ingest, after which they must be purged. This is set by the most recent policy update effective 2026-06-02 (history/2026-06.md line 4: "EU RAW payment logs retention reduced to 30 days post-ingest ... Raw personally-identifiable logs must be purged at 30 days"). That update supersedes the earlier policy of 7-year retention (e.g., history/2025-03.md line 94 and several other months), which now applies only to ANONYMIZED aggregates, not the raw PII logs.
