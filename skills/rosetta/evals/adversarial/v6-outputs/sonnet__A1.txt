A new EU enterprise billing service should use **ClickHouse** as its event-store backend. This was first decided on 2026-02-10 (history/2026-02.md): "billing event-store [eu/enterprise]: adopt ClickHouse for query throughput. Throughput, data-residency, cost, and migration risk were weighed before sign-off." The decision was explicitly reaffirmed on 2026-06-05 (history/2026-06.md): "Reaffirmed: EU enterprise billing event-store remains ClickHouse, effective immediately, all teams." The existing billing/services/eu_enterprise/store.py still reads ENGINE='self-hosted-postgres' (marked "# legacy"), confirming the current codebase has not yet been migrated and ClickHouse is the forward-looking standard. No subsequent decision overrides this choice, making ClickHouse the unambiguous current recommendation for any new EU enterprise billing service.
