The FRAUD service uses **protobuf** (Protocol Buffers) for internal messaging serialization. The decision chain is as follows: on 2026-03-20, the platform established a company-wide standard of "protobuf for all internal service-to-service messaging" (history/2026-03.md). On 2026-04-15, the ledger team explicitly aligned with that platform-wide standard (history/2026-04.md). Then on 2026-05-01, the fraud service (us/enterprise) adopted "the SAME format as the ledger team; see the ledger platform-alignment note" (history/2026-05.md), which by transitivity resolves to protobuf. While some region/tier-specific fraud entries from earlier dates mention other formats (e.g., msgpack for latam/enterprise in 2025-06, Avro for latam/free in 2026-02), the most authoritative and recent cross-cutting decision for the FRAUD service's internal messaging is protobuf, inherited from the platform standard via the ledger alignment.
