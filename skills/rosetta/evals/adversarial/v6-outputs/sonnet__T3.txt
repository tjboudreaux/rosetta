Raw EU payment logs (personally-identifiable) are retained for 30 days post-ingest, after which they must be purged. This policy was updated effective 2026-06-02, as recorded in history/2026-06.md: "EU RAW payment logs retention reduced to 30 days post-ingest; only ANONYMIZED aggregates are retained 7 years." The earlier entries in history/2025-03.md and history/2025-07.md citing 7-year retention referred to the overall compliance obligation, which now applies only to anonymized aggregates, not the raw PII logs.
