The FRAUD service uses protobuf for internal messaging. This is established through a chain of decisions: the platform serialization standard (2026-03-10) mandates protobuf for all internal service-to-service messaging. The ledger team aligned with this standard (2026-04-15), and the FRAUD service explicitly adopted the same format as the ledger team for serialization (2026-05-01). Therefore, FRAUD inherits protobuf as its internal messaging format via the platform standard and ledger alignment.
