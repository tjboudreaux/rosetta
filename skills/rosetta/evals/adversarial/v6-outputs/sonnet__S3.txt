The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (internally called "Project Nimbus") is the unified ledger tier, backed by Cassandra. As of 2026-06-08, the decision log states: "Project Nimbus migrates its primary store to the unified ledger tier for strong consistency, replacing the prior document store" (history/2026-06.md). This supersedes the earlier Spanner adoption recorded in March 2026 (history/2026-03.md: "profile datastore: adopt Spanner") and the May 2026 legacy note that DynamoDB tables were still present mid-migration (history/2026-05.md).
