The payments write-path uses NO automatic retry for write operations. This is an explicit exception to the platform-wide retry standard (exponential backoff with jitter), as documented in history/2026-05.md (entry dated 2026-05-20): "EXCEPTION to the retry standard: the payments write-path performs NO automatic retry." The rationale is that automatic retries risk double-charging customers; instead, idempotency keys are used to deduplicate writes, placing the responsibility for safe retry on the caller rather than the infrastructure.
