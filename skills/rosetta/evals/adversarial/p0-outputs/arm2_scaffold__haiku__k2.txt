The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (codename: Project Nimbus) is Cloud Spanner, specifically the unified ledger tier deployment. As of 2026-06-08, Project Nimbus migrated its primary store from DynamoDB (the prior document store) to the unified ledger tier for strong consistency. This decision replaced the legacy DynamoDB tables that were present during the migration period (noted in 2026-05-28).
