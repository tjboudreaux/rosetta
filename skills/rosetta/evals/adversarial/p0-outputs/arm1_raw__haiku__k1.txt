The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is Cloud Spanner (referred to as the "unified ledger tier" in the infrastructure glossary). As of 2026-06-08, Project Nimbus (the internal name for user-profile) migrated from its prior DynamoDB document store to Cloud Spanner for strong consistency. This migration is documented in the decision log (history/2025-09.md identifies the tier glossary, and history/2026-06.md records the migration decision) and represents a shift from eventual consistency to strong consistency guarantees.
