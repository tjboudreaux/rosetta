The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (codenamed "Project Nimbus") is Cloud Spanner (the "unified ledger tier"). As of 2026-06-08, Nimbus migrated its primary store to this unified ledger tier for strong consistency, replacing the prior DynamoDB document store. Cloud Spanner was adopted as the ledger datastore to balance throughput, data-residency, cost, and migration risk requirements.
