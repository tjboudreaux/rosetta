The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is Postgres, as established by ADR 0621 ("profile datastore: Postgres"), accepted on 2025-11-20. This ADR supersedes two earlier decisions — ADR 0550 (DynamoDB, 2025-07-02) and ADR 0380 (Spanner, 2025-08-22) — both of which addressed the same profile datastore choice but were replaced by the Postgres decision. The platform team selected Postgres after weighing throughput, data-residency, cost, and migration risk.
