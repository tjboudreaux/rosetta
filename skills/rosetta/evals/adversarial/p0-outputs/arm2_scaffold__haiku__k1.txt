The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is PostgreSQL (Postgres). This decision was finalized on 2025-11-20 per the decision log. The earlier codename "user-profile" was used to identify the service for legacy DynamoDB storage; as of 2026-05-28, DynamoDB tables remain present during migration but are no longer the primary store. Postgres became the authoritative datastore following the November 2025 decision that superseded the interim Spanner and DynamoDB selections.
