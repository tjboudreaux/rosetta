The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is **Postgres**, as established in ADR 0621 ("profile datastore: Postgres", accepted 2025-11-20). This supersedes two earlier decisions that had adopted Spanner (ADR 0380) and then DynamoDB (ADR 0550). The decision was made by the platform team after weighing throughput, data-residency requirements, cost, and migration risk.
