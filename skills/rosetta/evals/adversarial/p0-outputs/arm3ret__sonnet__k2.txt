The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (internally known as "Project Nimbus") is Cloud Spanner, referred to in project documentation as the "unified ledger tier." The most recent history entry (history/2026-06.md) records: "Project Nimbus migrates its primary store to the unified ledger tier for strong consistency, replacing the prior document store," and the project glossary confirms "the 'unified ledger tier' is our Cloud Spanner deployment" (history/2025-09.md). The prior datastore was DynamoDB — confirmed by history/2025-11.md and history/2026-05.md (which notes DynamoDB tables as legacy/still present during migration) — and profile/store.py corroborates the transition with ENGINE='dynamodb' explicitly commented as "legacy" and the file header warning "mid-migration; do not trust as current standard."
