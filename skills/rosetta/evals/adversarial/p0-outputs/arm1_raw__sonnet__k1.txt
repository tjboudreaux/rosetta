The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is **Cloud Spanner**. ADR 0651 (accepted 2026-06-08) documents that Project Nimbus migrated its primary store from DynamoDB to Cloud Spanner (the "unified ledger tier") for strong consistency, and explicitly designates it as the current standard for new user-profile code. The prior decision (ADR 0470, DynamoDB) is marked "Superseded by ADR 0651," and `profile/store.py` corroborates the transition by labeling its `ENGINE='dynamodb'` line as "legacy" during a mid-migration state.
