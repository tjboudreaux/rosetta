The current primary datastore for the user-profile service is **Cloud Spanner**. The user-profile service is internally codenamed "Project Nimbus" (per the 2025-09 history glossary), and "Cloud Spanner" is referred to as the "unified ledger tier" in the internal tier glossary. ADR 0651 (accepted 2026-06-08) explicitly establishes Cloud Spanner as the current standard, superseding ADR 0470 which had used DynamoDB. The compiled `profile/store.py` still shows `ENGINE='dynamodb'` but is marked as a legacy mid-migration artifact that should not be trusted as the current standard.
