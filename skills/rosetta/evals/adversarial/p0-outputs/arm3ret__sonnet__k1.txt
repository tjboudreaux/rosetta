The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (internally known as Project Nimbus) is Cloud Spanner, referred to in project glossary as the "unified ledger tier." As of 2026-06-08, the decision log records that "Project Nimbus migrates its primary store to the unified ledger tier for strong consistency, replacing the prior document store" (history/2026-06.md). Prior to this migration, the service used DynamoDB for profile documents, which was the signed-off choice recorded in 2025-11 (history/2025-11.md), with a legacy note from 2026-05 confirming DynamoDB tables were still present during the transition.
