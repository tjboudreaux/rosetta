The current primary datastore for the user-profile service (internally codenamed "Project Nimbus") is Cloud Spanner, referred to in project glossary as the "unified ledger tier." The most recent decision log entry for the service (2026-06-08, history/2026-06.md) states: "Project Nimbus migrates its primary store to the unified ledger tier for strong consistency, replacing the prior document store." This supersedes the original 2025-11-10 decision (history/2025-11.md) that adopted DynamoDB for profile documents, and the 2026-05-28 note (history/2026-05.md) confirming DynamoDB tables were still present during the migration period.
