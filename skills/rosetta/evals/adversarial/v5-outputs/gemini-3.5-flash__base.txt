Today, a new EU enterprise billing service must use **CloudSQL Postgres** (the "managed relational tier") as its event-store backend. According to the engineering decision log from June 12, 2026 (`history/2026-06.md`), Project Meridian persistence was migrated to the managed relational tier to satisfy GDPR data-residency requirements, replacing the prior columnar backend (ClickHouse). This mandate is explicitly designated as "Effective for all GDPR-zone tier-1 sinks going forward," superseding the previous ClickHouse reaffirmation from June 5, 2026. This connection is confirmed by the September 1, 2025 decision log (`history/2025-09.md`), which defines the codename "Project Meridian" as the EU enterprise billing event sink and the "managed relational tier" as CloudSQL Postgres.
