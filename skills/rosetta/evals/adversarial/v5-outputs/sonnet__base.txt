A new EU enterprise billing service should use ClickHouse as its event-store backend. The engineering decision log records this choice consistently across multiple reviews: ClickHouse was first selected for the EU enterprise billing event-store on 2025-04-28 ("for analytics throughput"), reaffirmed as the standard on 2026-02-10 ("Chosen for query throughput"), confirmed again on 2026-04-21 and 2026-05-20, and most recently on 2026-06-05 the decision was explicitly reaffirmed: "EU enterprise billing event-store remains ClickHouse, effective immediately, all teams." The existing eu_enterprise/store.py file is marked as a legacy pre-migration service still on self-hosted Postgres, but the current architectural standard mandates ClickHouse, selected over candidates including DynamoDB, self-hosted Postgres, and BigQuery based on throughput, data-residency, cost, and migration risk criteria.
