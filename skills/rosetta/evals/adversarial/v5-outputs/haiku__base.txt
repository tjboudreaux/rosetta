A new EU enterprise billing service should use ClickHouse for its event-store backend. ClickHouse was selected as the standard for EU enterprise billing event-stores on February 10, 2026, specifically chosen for query throughput and analytics performance after weighing throughput, data-residency, cost, and migration risk across candidate options. This decision was reaffirmed as recently as June 5, 2026, confirming it remains the current standard for all EU enterprise billing services and is effective immediately organization-wide.
