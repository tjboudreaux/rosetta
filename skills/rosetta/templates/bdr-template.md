## Context

The business / strategy / commercial forces in play: market, cost, risk, partnership, hiring, spend,
positioning, or compliance. A BDR captures a *business* decision — often made by humans in a meeting
or thread, not in code — so it doesn't evaporate when the conversation scrolls away.

## Decision

What we will do, stated plainly.

## Business impact

The expected effect on cost, revenue, risk, headcount, timeline, or strategic position. State
assumptions and how the decision will be judged in hindsight.
